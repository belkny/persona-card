Jaden (The Hustler)

Gig Worker

Jaden is a 23 year old gig worker with variable income.
His credit is erratic but he has proven to be pretty resourceful,
though he is in need of guidance.

Goals

  • Stabilize his cash flow
  • Raise his credit limit
  • Pay off his credit card debt

Frustrations

  • He has to spend money to make money - Business expenses. I bought a MacBook and a camera. I'm paying it off, but the interest is killing me. It accumulated because I needed the gear to get the jobs to pay for the gear. Catch-22.
  • His utilization spikes when he buys equipment - My goal is to get a higher credit limit so my utilization doesn't spike every time I buy equipment.
  • Hidden fees - It tells me exactly how much interest I'll pay if I don't pay in full, which I love. It's transparent. Other banks hide that info.
